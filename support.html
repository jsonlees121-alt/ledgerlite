FAQ 常见问题

Does data sync to the cloud? 数据会同步到云端吗？
No. All data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage (NSUserDefaults). Nothing is uploaded to any server. 不会。简记的所有数据都存储在你的设备本地，使用 iOS 系统的安全存储机制（NSUserDefaults），不会上传到任何服务器。
Will I lose data when switching phones? 换手机后数据会丢失吗？
Yes. Since data is fully local, it won't auto-sync to a new phone. We recommend exporting a CSV backup before switching devices. 是的。因为数据完全本地存储，换新手机不会自动同步。建议在换机前用「导出账单」功能备份 CSV 文件。
Why isn't the reminder working? 记账提醒不工作怎么办？
Check: 1) Settings → Notifications → LedgerLite → Allow; 2) The app must be running in background; 3) Reminder triggers only at the set time. 请检查：1) 设置 → 通知 → 简记 → 允许通知；2) App 需要在后台运行才能收到提醒；3) 提醒时间需到点才会触发。
How to edit or delete a transaction? 如何修改或删除交易？
On the Transactions page: long-press a transaction to edit (amount, note, date, type); swipe left to reveal the delete button. 在「明细」页：长按某条交易可修改（金额、备注、日期、收支类型）；向左滑动交易可显示删除按钮。
Where is the exported CSV? 导出的 CSV 在哪里？
Tap "Export Bills" on the Settings page. The system share sheet will appear — save to Files, send via email, or share to other apps. 在「我的」页点「导出账单到本地」，会触发系统分享面板，你可以保存到文件 App、发到微信或邮件。
Is the app free? Any in-app purchases? App 收费吗？有内购吗？
LedgerLite is completely free, no in-app purchases, no ads, all features free forever. 简记完全免费，无任何内购，无广告，永久免费使用所有功能。
